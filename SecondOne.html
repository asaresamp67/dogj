DOG-Jesus

Daughters of Glorious Jesus is a Ghanaian musical trio composed of women namely Cynthia Appiadu, Edna Sarpong and Monica Owusu Ansah. They are a gospel group who sing in English and Twi. With over fifty songs still with strong appeal to audience, DoG-J has the most number of songs by a Ghanaian Gospel Artiste that is on the list of all time favourites.
